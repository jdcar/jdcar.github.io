Jamie Carlstone

Prior to my current role, I worked at the University of Chicago and the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Since October 2019 I have been the Authority Control Librarian at Northwestern University.

Research interests include serials, assessment, and copyright renewal. I am particularly interested in finding ways to use batch processes to improve catalog metadata.

When I'm not working, I enjoying doing DIY projects, playing guitar, and spending time with my dog Studs.
